About Lynn

It was not an easy decision for Lynn to switch her career over from an art museum professional with 8+ years of experience to a UX/UI Designer but she proudly says it was one of the best decisions she has eer made in her life. She is glad that she followed what her hearted desired, improving people's lives by solving their real-life problems with her skills and passion in UX/UI Design.


Lynn's whole life has been revolving around art, from studio art to art history. And now, she is creating art that is based on research, logic, and meanings. Her background in studio art and art history equally magnifies her ability in UI Design and UX design.


She is temporarily based in Washington D.C. area but planning to move back to New York City in 2021. When she is not working, she loves to watch movies, play golf, search good songs, and take photographs of little things that make her day beautiful.


Highlights of Her Skills

User Research & User Empathy

Wireframing

Where the research date, ideas, and visual foundation come together. The most attractive part of wireframing is that it allows you to be creative yet with some guidelines and boundaries.
UI Design

Enhance the usability of a product with a coherent design. The visual aesthetics are also backed up by logic and reasoning. Explore and play with colors, design, and animations.
